# E-Commerce Sales Analysis — Key Business Insights

##  1. Revenue Concentration by Category

The category *cama_mesa_banho* generates the highest revenue (~3000+), followed by *esporte_lazer* and *moveis_decoracao*. This indicates that a few categories contribute significantly to total sales.

---

##  2. Revenue Dominated by São Paulo (SP)

The state *SP* contributes the highest revenue (over 5 million), far exceeding other states. This highlights a strong regional dependency on São Paulo.

---

##  3. Top Cities Driving Customer Base

Cities like *São Bernardo do Campo* and *Rio de Janeiro* have the highest number of customers (15,000+ and 6,000+ respectively), making them key target markets.

---

##  4. Payment Method Preference

Over **75% of transactions** are made using **credit cards (~76,000 transactions)**, followed by *boleto (~19,000)*. Other payment methods have minimal usage.

---

##  5. Order Fulfillment Efficiency

The majority of orders are successfully delivered (~97,000+), while cancelled or unavailable orders are significantly lower, indicating efficient order processing.

---

##  6. Sales Trend Over Time

Monthly revenue shows an increasing trend over time, with peak months crossing **1 million+ in revenue**, suggesting business growth and possible seasonal spikes.

---

##  7. Customer Spending Distribution

A small group of customers contributes disproportionately to total revenue, with top customers spending significantly higher than the average.

---

##  8. Revenue Distribution Across Regions

Revenue is unevenly distributed across states, with a few states contributing most of the sales while others show relatively low performance.

---

##  9. Business Dependency on Key Segments

The business heavily depends on:

* A few top-performing product categories
* A limited number of high-revenue states
* Credit card payments

This indicates potential risk if these segments decline.

---

##  10. Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing on high-performing categories
* Expand operations in high-revenue states like SP
* Improve growth in low-performing regions
* Optimize payment experience for dominant methods
* Target high-value customers for retention strategies
